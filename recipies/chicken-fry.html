Fried Chicken

Description

Fried Chicken,also known as Southern fried chicken, is a dish consisting of chicken pieces
that have been coated with seasoned flour or batter and pan-fried, deep fried, pressure fried, or air fried.

Ingredients

  • Chicken meat
  • Oil
  • Water
  • Minced garlic
  • Coriander powder
  • Chili powder
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Prep: Cut chicken, pat dry for crispy skin. (Marinating is optional).
  2. Dredge: Set up bowls for seasoned flour, wet dredge (buttermilk or eggs), and another flour coating. Dredge chicken in each bowl.
  3. Fry: Heat oil to 350°F. Fry chicken in batches until golden brown and cooked through (165°F internal temp).
  4. Drain & Rest: Drain on paper towels, then let rest for a few minutes before serving for juicy chicken.
  5. Enjoy! Dip in your favorite sauce and devour!
