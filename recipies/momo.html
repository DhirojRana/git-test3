Momo

Description

Momos are a type of steamed filled dumpling in Tibetan and Nepali cuisine that is also popular in neighbouring Bhutan and India.
Momos are usually served with a sauce known as achar influenced by the spices and herbs used within many South Asian.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Ground buffalo meat
  • Chopped Onions
  • Minced garlic
  • Coriander powder
  • Chili powder
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix filling: Combine ground meat/veggies, chopped aromatics, spices, and oil.
  2. Make dough (or buy wrappers): Knead flour and water, rest dough.
  3. Assemble: Roll dough thin, fill, and pleat.
  4. Steam: Steam momos until cooked through.
  5. Serve: Enjoy hot with a dipping sauce.
